# Project Objectives

## General Objective
To explore the use of Generative AI as a tool for active learning and knowledge organization through the creation of a structured study guide on personal financial planning.

## Specific Objectives
- Understand the basic principles of personal financial planning
- Analyze key concepts such as expense control, budgeting, emergency funds, and financial goals
- Test different prompt variations to evaluate how AI responses change based on instruction
- Produce structured summaries and a financial glossary
- Create reusable prompts to support future learning and revision
