# Summaries

## Personal Financial Planning
Personal financial planning is the process of organizing income and expenses to achieve financial stability and long-term goals. It involves understanding how money is earned, spent, and saved.

## Expense Control
Expense control refers to tracking and managing spending to ensure expenses do not exceed income. It helps identify unnecessary costs and supports better financial decisions.

## Monthly Budget
A monthly budget is a financial plan that outlines expected income and expenses for a specific month. It helps allocate money to essential needs, savings, and goals.

## Emergency Fund
An emergency fund is a financial reserve designed to cover unexpected expenses such as medical emergencies or job loss. It provides security and reduces reliance on credit.
