# Text Sources

The following open-access text sources were used to support the study:

- Articles on personal financial planning and budgeting from financial education platforms
- Educational materials from financial institutions and consumer education initiatives
- Introductory texts explaining expense control, emergency funds, and financial goals
- Reference articles on basic financial concepts such as budgeting and planning

All sources were selected for their clarity, accessibility, and educational focus.
